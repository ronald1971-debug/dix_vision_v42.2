## CACHE POLICY OPTIMIZATION

### **Compliance-Weighted TTL**

#### **TTL Adjustment Rules**
- **High Compliance (80%+)**: TTL increased by 50%
- **Medium Compliance (50-79%)**: Standard TTL
- **Low Compliance (< 50%)**: TTL decreased by 50%

#### **Examples**
- **Crypto Prices** (30s base): 15s (low) → 30s (medium) → 45s (high)
- **News** (300s base): 150s (low) → 300s (medium) → 450s (high)
- **Institutional** (86400s base): 43200s (low) → 86400s (medium) → 129600s (high)
